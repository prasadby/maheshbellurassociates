About Us

Mahesh V Bellur

Mahesh V Bellur is a first generation Entrepreneur , and before establishing his private label firm specialized in Business Management training and executive coaching, he worked with MNCs and startup eco system in Senior Business Leadership roles covering pan India / SAARC and middle east region.

An Electronics & Communication Engineer by Education, Mahesh is a trained Business Manager through Harvard Business School thanks to executive sponsorship from his ex employer, and has completed Corporate Leadership Training though IIMB Executive Education Program.
He is also an independent technology consultant to few Consulting firms in market research space.

Widely travelled and having worked with people from multi cultures, Mahesh Bellur is considered an industry expert with fantastic communication and motivation skills in areas of Sales Management, Business strategy, Leadership training and mentoring skills.
He has helped many organizations in redefining business strategy, trained sales teams for success, and he is associated with quite a few startups in scaling up productivity, by designing an organizational structure, sales process, talent retention and Appraisal mechanism.

Mahesh has addressed students at many professional colleges as a guest speaker and trainer, thereby guiding student community in career planning
